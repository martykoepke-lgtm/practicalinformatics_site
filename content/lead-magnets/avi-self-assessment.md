# The AI Visibility Self-Assessment

## Score your business in 5 minutes

A six-dimension scorecard for established small businesses.

By Practical Informatics — practicalinformatics.com

---

## Why this matters

Something quietly changed in the last 18 months: a meaningful share of your buyers stopped Googling and started asking ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity for recommendations.

- **73% of B2B buyers now research vendors using AI agents.** *(Forrester, 2026)*
- **AI search traffic grew 42.8% year-over-year** while Google grew 2.4%. *(SimilarWeb, 2026)*
- Most of this happens **invisibly**. The customer asks AI, gets a recommendation (or doesn't), and you never see the visit in your analytics.

When your buyer asks an AI agent for a recommendation in your category, you are either named — or you're not. If you're not, you've lost a lead you never knew existed.

This scorecard takes about five minutes. It tells you where you stand across the six dimensions that determine whether AI knows, recommends, and accurately describes your business.

It's a starting point, not a final answer. The real audit — what AI actually says about you, in real time — is the AI Visibility Index. The link is on the last page.

---

## How to use this scorecard

- **18 questions**, mostly yes / no
- **5 points possible per dimension**, **30 total**
- Be honest — there's no prize for a high score, only useful information
- Tally your points as you go in the score box at the end of each dimension
- At the end, find your tier and decide what to do next

A note: most established small businesses score between **8 and 18** their first time through. If you score lower, that's not failure — it's a starting line.

# The Six Dimensions

## Dimension 1: Founder Credibility (out of 5)

*Does AI know who's behind the business — and trust the description it has?*

**Q1. (1 pt)** Is your name (or your founder's name) on the home page of your website?

☐ Yes — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

**Q2. (2 pts)** Is your LinkedIn profile linked from your website, with a consistent name, role, and bio?

☐ Yes, consistent — 2 pts  ☐ Linked but inconsistent — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

**Q3. (2 pts)** If someone asked an AI agent "Who is [your name] of [your business]?", would the answer be accurate, current (last 12 months), and include at least one credibility marker (book, education, certifications, prior roles)?

☐ Yes, mostly — 2 pts  ☐ Partly accurate — 1 pt  ☐ No, or AI doesn't know — 0 pts

> **Dimension 1 score: ___ / 5**

## Dimension 2: Live AI Test (out of 5)

*Have you asked AI what it says — and did you like the answer?*

**Q1. (1 pt)** Have you asked ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini "Tell me about [your business name]" in the last 90 days?

☐ Yes — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

**Q2. (2 pts)** When you ask an AI agent "Best [your industry / category] in [your location or specialty]," does it ever name your business?

☐ Yes, often — 2 pts  ☐ Sometimes, with prompting — 1 pt  ☐ Never — 0 pts

**Q3. (2 pts)** When AI describes your business, is the description accurate — including what you do, who you serve, and at least one current detail (pricing, named methodology, recent work)?

☐ Yes, accurate — 2 pts  ☐ Partly accurate — 1 pt  ☐ Inaccurate or missing — 0 pts

> **Dimension 2 score: ___ / 5**

---

## Dimension 3: Entity Clarity (out of 5)

*Can AI tell exactly who and where you are — without confusion?*

**Q1. (1 pt)** Is your business name, address, and phone (NAP) consistent across your website, Google Business Profile, and LinkedIn?

☐ Yes — 1 pt  ☐ No / not all set up — 0 pts

**Q2. (2 pts)** Is there another business with a similar name (different city, different industry, similar spelling) that AI might confuse you with?

☐ No, my name is unique — 2 pts  ☐ Maybe, at least one similar-named entity — 1 pt  ☐ Yes, several — 0 pts

**Q3. (2 pts)** Can a stranger find a clear one-sentence description of what your business does, on your home page, in under 3 seconds?

☐ Yes, instantly — 2 pts  ☐ Yes, but they'd have to scroll — 1 pt  ☐ Not really — 0 pts

> **Dimension 3 score: ___ / 5**

---

## Dimension 4: Methodology Depth (out of 5)

*Does your offering have a name and a clear shape AI can repeat?*

**Q1. (1 pt)** Does your business have a named methodology, framework, or signature process (something with a unique 2–4 word name)?

☐ Yes — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

**Q2. (2 pts)** Is your pricing visible on your website — at least a range or starting point?

☐ Yes, specific — 2 pts  ☐ Yes, vague ("call for quote") — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

**Q3. (2 pts)** Does your site have a clear "who this is for" and "who this isn't for" section?

☐ Both, clearly — 2 pts  ☐ One but not the other — 1 pt  ☐ Neither — 0 pts

> **Dimension 4 score: ___ / 5**

---

## Dimension 5: Structured Data (out of 5)

*Does your site speak the machine-readable language LLMs use?*

**Q1. (1 pt)** Does your site have an `llms.txt` file? Check by visiting `yourdomain.com/llms.txt`.

☐ Yes — 1 pt  ☐ No / not sure — 0 pts

**Q2. (2 pts)** Does your site have JSON-LD schema markup for your Organization or LocalBusiness? Check by visiting your home page, right-clicking → "View Page Source" → searching for `application/ld+json`.

☐ Yes, present and complete — 2 pts  ☐ Yes, but minimal — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

**Q3. (2 pts)** Does your site have a real FAQ page with questions written the way your buyers actually ask them (e.g., "How much does X cost?" not "Pricing Inquiries")?

☐ Yes, with FAQ schema — 2 pts  ☐ Yes, but no schema markup — 1 pt  ☐ No FAQ at all — 0 pts

> **Dimension 5 score: ___ / 5**

---

## Dimension 6: Agent and Citation Graph (out of 5)

*Does the wider web confirm what your site says about you?*

**Q1. (1 pt)** Does your `robots.txt` explicitly allow `GPTBot`, `ClaudeBot`, or `PerplexityBot`? Check at `yourdomain.com/robots.txt`.

☐ Yes — 1 pt  ☐ No / not sure / blocking by default — 0 pts

**Q2. (2 pts)** Are you mentioned by name on at least three external websites (industry directories, press, podcasts, guest posts) that you don't own or control?

☐ Yes, three or more — 2 pts  ☐ One or two — 1 pt  ☐ None — 0 pts

**Q3. (2 pts)** Do you have a Wikipedia or Wikidata entry — or at least a high-authority mention (named industry publication, well-known podcast, recognized conference)?

☐ Yes — 2 pts  ☐ Maybe / niche mentions — 1 pt  ☐ No — 0 pts

> **Dimension 6 score: ___ / 5**
